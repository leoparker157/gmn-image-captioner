# Privacy Policy for GMN Image Captioner

**Last Updated: April 11, 2026**

GMN Image Captioner ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
The Extension captures image data (URLs or pixel data) from web pages **only** when you explicitly request a caption using the "Caption with GMN" command or by pasting/uploading an image into the interface.

## 2. Data Processing
To provide its core functionality, the Extension sends the captured image data and your custom prompts to the **Google Gemini AI API**. By using the Extension, you acknowledge that your data will be processed by Google's AI services according to their own privacy policies.

## 3. Data Storage
*   **API Keys**: Your Google Gemini API key is stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. It is never sent to our servers.
*   **Presets and Settings**: All custom prompts, presets, and configuration settings are stored locally on your device.
*   **Images**: The Extension does not store the images you caption. They are held in memory only for the duration of the request to the AI API.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. Data is only transmitted to Google (as the AI service provider) to fulfill your request for an image caption.

## 5. User Control
You can delete all stored data (API keys and settings) at any time by:
1. Clearing the extension storage in the settings panel.
2. Uninstalling the Extension.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the repository.

## 7. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the GitHub repository issues page.
